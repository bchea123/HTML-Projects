Parking Brake:


The Parking Brake, also known as an Emergency Brake, is used to park a manual transmission vehicle. Unlike a vehicle with an automatic transmission, vehicles with a manual transmission do not have a parking gear.

To park a manual transmission, activate the parking brake by pulling upwards on the lever.
Note: You can shift the Gear Box into any gear to ensure that the transmission locks the driving wheels of the vehicle. This is best practice while parking on sloped hills to ensure the vehicle does not roll away.
