 About the Instructor:

Hello there, and welcome to my webpage on Manual Transmissions! My name is Brandon Chea and I am 22 years old, born and raised in the San Gabriel Valley in California! I am looking to instruct students in my own personal vehicle on how to drive manual transmission to earn a little extra money for college. I have a little over 5 years of experience driving manual transmission vehicles for both work and personal use. My first manual transmission vehicle was a 1989 Ford Mustang GT 5.0 Convertible. I bought the car to learn how to drive manual transmission. Afterwards, I sold the convertible for a 2001 V6 Ford Mustang in Electric Green. In this car I was able to fine tune my experience driving a manual transmission vehicle and began learning advanced techniques. Currently, I am now driving a 2014 V6 Ford Mustang in Shadow Black.
